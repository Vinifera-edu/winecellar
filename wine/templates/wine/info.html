Welcome to AI Wine Cellar

A modern app for wine lovers who want to keep a detailed, searchable, and AI-enhanced log of their bottles. Manage your personal cellar, track purchase history, see drink windows, and explore what's next with AI pairing.

🚀 Features
  • Register and manage wines
  • Automatic sorting by drinkability, country, region, and vintage
  • Instant search and table filters
  • Sortable columns
  • Inline wine detail modals
  • Wine log with history, edits and stock tracking
  • Export to CSV & Excel
